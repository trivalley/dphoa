CC&R’s

Declaration of Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions

When you buy your house in Danville Place, the title company handling your escrow must give you a copy of the CC&Rs. It is important that every homeowner be aware of the Covenants, Conditions and Restrictions which affect his/her property. Your Association is run strictly in accordance with the CC&Rs and reading them will give you a better understanding of the Association.

Our Architectural Control Committee reminds all residents of the covenants and restrictions regarding any alterations, repairs or additions to residences. No owner shall make structural alterations, repairs or additions which would substantially alter the exterior appearance thereof without the prior written approval of the plans and specifications by the committee. All plans are to be submitted to Howe Association Management. You can download an application here. You’ll need a login and password to enter. You can get it from the quarterly news letter or contact the management office..
